    Our Mission

    There is a new wave questioning the “real-ness” of birds, and this club seeks to combat the false claims made by said nonbelievers. We are dedicated to teaching our core values: the importance of venturing into nature, the fact that birds are real, and the healing power of bird watching.

    We urge you to join us today and to see for yourself that birds are real!
